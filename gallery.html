About Us

Our mission is to be Earth’s most customer-centric company. Hindsight is guided by four principles: customer obsession rather than competitor focus, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and long-term thinking.

Although our business started a year ago during a pandemic, our goal is the customers’ desire for lower prices, better selection, and convenient services. Today, Hindsight can find what they’re looking for online and in person. From delivering fresh produce to their doorstep to creating and distributing movies, music, and more, we are always finding new ways to delight our customers.
